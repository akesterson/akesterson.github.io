My current role is Cloud Engineering Manager at Wiser Solutions, Inc. In total, I have 16 years (+/-) experience in engineering and technology roles. 7+ of those years have been spent managing devops/SRE/hosting teams responsible for critical revenue-generating services, while the other 8 were spent in engineering roles building out platforms, services, and workflows. 5+ of those years have been spent working on cloud and hybrid infrastructures, especially solving problems of growth and scale, and automating the same.

I use relationships and business networks to form partnerships to build and operate teams, business functions and services that enable and improve the business. I have a strong bias to action, tempered with humility and a thorough commitment to data-driven decision making. I excel and build teams that excel in fully remote environments; I grew up in the open source community where none of us ever existed in the same place at the same time but still managed to change the world of software. I intentionally develop all people within my reach, nourishing and encouraging their growth through 1on1 meetings and actionable appropriate feedback.
